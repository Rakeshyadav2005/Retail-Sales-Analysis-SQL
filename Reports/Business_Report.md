# Retail Sales Analysis - Business Report

## Project Summary

This report presents key business insights generated from the Retail Sales Analysis SQL project. The analysis is based on customer, product, and order data stored in a relational MySQL database.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify top-performing products.
* Evaluate revenue generation.
* Monitor order status and payment trends.
* Generate actionable business insights using SQL.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 1. Total Revenue

Calculates the total sales revenue generated from all completed orders.

### 2. Average Order Value

Determines the average monetary value of each customer order.

### 3. Best Selling Product

Identifies the product with the highest quantity sold.

### 4. Top Spending Customer

Finds the customer who generated the highest revenue.

### 5. Revenue by Product Category

Compares revenue across different product categories.

### 6. Revenue by Payment Method

Analyzes customer payment preferences.

### 7. Monthly Sales Trend

Tracks monthly revenue to identify seasonal trends.

### 8. Order Status Analysis

Summarizes Delivered, Shipped, Cancelled, and Returned orders.
